The Legend of Ergenekon

Out to the World

The blacksmith came back to the people and explained how they will get out of the valley. They worked all together to melt the mountain and open the path to freedom. When the mountain was melt, Asena lead the Turks into the world.

Getting Outside of the Ergenekon, lead by Asena

The people led out of the valley found the Turkic Khaganate, in which the valley functions as its capital.
Up to this day, every year the Turks celebrate the legendary ancestral escape from Ergenekon, which represents their stive and struggle for freedom.
