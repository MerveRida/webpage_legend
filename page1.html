The Legend of Ergenekon

Coming into the Ergenekon Valley

Following a military defeat, the ancient Turks took refuge in the legendary Ergenekon valley.

A scene from Altai Mountains

The Ergenekon Valley was surrounded by the large Altai mountains, and was very safe because there was no way to come in.
However, that meant that there was also no way out, so the Turks were stuck in the Ergenekon Valley for four centuries.
